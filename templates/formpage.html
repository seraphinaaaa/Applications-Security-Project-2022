How do I start blogging?

If you’re looking for a quick guide on how to start a blog and make money online, here are the eight steps you should take!

Choose the niche for your blog. Go for something that you’re passionate about. Ideally, it should be a topic that will interest others as well. Get web hosting and a domain name. Purchase hosting that best suits your budget and needs. Then, come up with a custom name for your domain, check its availability, and register it through a domain registrar. Set up a platform for your blog. Choose a platform and set it up so you can start publishing content. Create your blog with WordPress. We have chosen this platform as it is the most popular one. Design and customize your blog. Change its design and functionality to your liking. Publish content. Familiarize yourself with the available publishing tools and start planning, writing, and posting articles on your blog. Promote and grow the following of your blog. Strategize and execute ways to promote your blog to increase its traffic and gain loyal readers. Monetize your blog. Once you have sizable traffic and readership, implement methods like ads and affiliate marketing.

In the upcoming sections, we’ll break down each step in the journey on how to make a blog for beginners and provide more details.

Why should you start blogging?

People start blogs for all sorts of reasons…

To educate, to have a voice, to make money. It’s important to understand your driving force. The reason you start is the reason you will keep going. The reason you will get up every morning at 4am while everyone else is happily sleeping, to write your next inspired piece of content. For me, it was about the power of the social web. My blog was a publishing machine that gave me a voice to the masses, it gave my audience a voice, it helped the little guy get noticed in a world of inflated egos and celebrity clickbait headlines. Blogging helped me make some noise and leave a little dent on this universe.

But it’s also more than that. Above all else, blogging is a platform for self development.

How do I be more confident when blogging?

Fear is the great confidence killer. Blogging with love infuses confidence into your being. Blog with love. Face fear-drivers. Stop blogging only to make money or to be popular. Blog mainly to help people. Removing fear focused on an obsession with blogging outcomes does wonders for boosting your self-belief. Make this shift to become a more confident blogger.

Blogging mainly for fun moves you out of the doubt-filled “OK, how am I doing?” energy so you can create from the clear, bold, “Wow this is fun!” energy. Make fun not metrics your prime driver. Blogging for fun removes heavy, leaden, judgment-based ways of blogging, and leaves ’em in the rear view mirror.

Diving into blogging fears and life fears builds blogging confidence like few other practices. Being more fearless – and willing to face fear – leaves doubts behind you as you evolve into a clear, confident blogger. Get comfortable with being uncomfortable. Broadcast live. Write and self-publish your first eBook. Do what you fear doing to increase blogging confidence.
